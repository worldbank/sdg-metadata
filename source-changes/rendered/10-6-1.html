Name:

FFD/UN-DESA

Description:

The data is compiled and the proportions calculated by the Financing for Sustainable Development Office, United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs.

4.b. Comment and limitations (REC_USE_LIM)

Cross institutional comparisons needsneed to pay attention to the different membership of the institutions. Voting rights and membership in their institutions are agreed by the Member States themselves. As a structural indicator, there will be only small changes over time to reflect agreement on new States joining as Members, suspension of voting rights, membership withdrawal and negotiated voting rights changes.

5. Data availability and disaggregation (COVERAGE)

Data availability:

Time series:

2000, 2005, 2010, 2015, and annually thereafter

Disaggregation:

Data is calculated and presented separately for each international organization.
