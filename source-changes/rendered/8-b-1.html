4.b. Comment and limitations (REC_USE_LIM)

 Governments may have de facto national strategies for youth employment, but lack an officially adopted de jure document. For SDG 8.b.1 monitoring purposes only what emerges from de jure documents is considered.

5. Data availability and disaggregation (COVERAGE)

Data availability:

The methodology is mainly based on a methodology used for the ILO youth employment policies database (YouthPOL) that covers 65145 countries in 5 regions, including: Colombia, Mexico, Jordan, Australia, Cambodia, China, Republic of Korea, Philippines, Germany, Kazakhstan, Russian Federation, Italy, Spain, and Ukraine. The data can be accessed in this link. The methodology is based on a simplified version of the questionnaires used in this database.

Time series: This submission covers data from 2019 to 2020.
