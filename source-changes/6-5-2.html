It is derived by adding up the surface area in a country of those transboundary surface water catchments and transboundary aquifers (i.e. &#x2018;transboundary&#x2019; basins) that are covered by an operational arrangement and dividing the obtained area by the aggregate total area in a country of all transboundary basins (both catchments and aquifers). The result is multiplied by 100 to obtain it expressed as percentage share.</p>
